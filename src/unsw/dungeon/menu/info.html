How To Play

Movement

Use the arrow keys to move around.

Goals

Complete the goals shown on the left hand side of the screen to complete the dungeon.

Keys + Doors

Collect keys to unlock doors and progress through the dungeon.

Portals

Portals allow you to quickly move across the dungeon by teleporting between two paired portals.

Enemies

Beware of enemies! You will die upon touching an enemy.

Swords + Potions

Use these to defeat enemies.

Visibility

You can only see your immediate surroundings so beware when navigating the dungeon.
